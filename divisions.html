Core Business Divisions

1. LTV Tech & Robotics

  • Develop AI-driven and robotic solutions for industrial and urban problems.
  • Design crime prevention gadgets (e.g., smart surveillance drones, AI policing systems).
  • Traffic management systems using IoT and automation.

2. LTV Green Energy

  • Research and implement alternative electricity generation (solar hybrids, kinetic energy, waste-to-power).
  • Combat load-shedding through decentralized micro-grid solutions.
  • Recycling old energy systems into efficient, eco-friendly power sources.

3. LTV Academy

  • Coding & programming boot camps for youth.
  • Robotics and AI workshops.
  • Partnerships with schools, governments, and tech hubs for skills development.

4. LTV Smart Cities Initiative

  • Water purification and distribution tech.
  • Solar-powered street lighting and smart grids.
  • Waste management automation.
